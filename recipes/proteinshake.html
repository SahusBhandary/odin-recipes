Protein Shake

Description

This is a protein shake with over 60g of protein. It is comprised of berries, bannans, peanut butter, and more. The only tools you will need is a blender and spoon.

Ingredients

  • Bannana
  • Berries
  • Peanut Butter
  • Whey Protein Powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Cut the bannana and put it into the blender bottle
  2. Add a cup of berries into the blender bottle
  3. Add a spoon of peanut butter into the blender bottle
  4. Add two scoops of whey protein powder into the blender bottle
  5. Finally add around 2 cups of milk into the blender
  6. Blend and enjoy!
