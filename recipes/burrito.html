Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Seasoning
  • Tortilla
  • Cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Put the chicken, rice, and beans into a pan
  2. Apply a lot of taco seasoning
  3. Place the burrito mix and cheese onto the tortilla
  4. Wrap the tortilla
  5. Place onto the stove and cook until the cheese is melted
